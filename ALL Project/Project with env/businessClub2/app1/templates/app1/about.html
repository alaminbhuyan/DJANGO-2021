General Code Of Conduct

  1. The definition of Club Member: Any Student of the School of Business has registered himself/herself as a member by applying in the specified form of the club.
  2. All Club Members must observe the highest standard of behevior and act responsibly when representing the club or attending and event/activity organized by this or any other Club
  3. A Club member who cannot maintain the expected level of behavior may be subject to disciplinary action. For example, Misuse of the power given to him from the club.
  4. No remarks should be used cr discrimination should be made based on seniority, race, religion, origin, gender, ability, or age.
  5. Following set of behaviors are also expectd from Club Members:
    1. Exhibit good attendance and punctuality in all club events;
    2. Follow the chain-of-command of the club;
    3. Show respect to any decision of the club;
    4. Dress in business oufit in any formal event/meeting of the club;
    5. Do not use club property/fund for personal benefit;
  6. Every Officer and member is expected to perform his/her given task and duties accordingly. Failure to meet the deadlines may result in termination from the posts.
  7. All the members are required to have utmost precautionary measure so tht the image of the school of Business is not hampered.
